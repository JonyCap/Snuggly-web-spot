FAQ

Common questions about the AI DevLab Setup Service.

Is this just a feature of your SaaS product?
No. The AI DevLab Setup Service is a separate service line. It involves physical furniture, hardware, and consulting work. The SaaS product is still our original cloud platform, sold on its own subscription.
Can clients use the DevLab Setup Service without your software?
Yes. A client could ask for an AI DevLab setup even if they currently use other AI tools. However, our recommended bundle always includes a connection to our SaaS, and we see it as the best way to get full value from the studio.
How long does the setup take?
For the Starter and Growth Lab packages, we design and deliver the DevLab within roughly 7 days (depending on delivery times). Enterprise setups may take longer because of custom requirements.
Do you handle licenses for the software tools?
We help clients choose tools, estimate license costs, and configure accounts. Final license ownership stays with the client, but we make sure everything is ready to use on the onboarding day.
Who is this service designed for?
Small tech companies, product teams, and startups that want a focused AI lab environment without spending weeks planning hardware, furniture, and software on their own.
What happens after the DevLab is installed?
After the onboarding workshop, teams can keep using the DevLab every day. We stay available through our SaaS support channels for questions related to the platform and the connected tool stack.
