Gemini Courier: Route Challenge

Mission Brief

You are a Gemini Courier pilot tasked with collecting parcels from 5 planets and returning to your base (GemHQ). Your goal is to select the most efficient route — lowest total cost — by clicking planets in the order you wish to visit them.

How to Play

  • Click on each planet once to add it to your route.
  • You must visit all planets and return to your starting point (GemHQ).
  • Your total route cost will be shown when you're done.
  • Try different routes to beat your best score!
