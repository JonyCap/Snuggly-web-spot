Gemini Courier: Elite Delivery

Mission Brief

You are a Gemini Courier pilot tasked with collecting parcels from distant planets and returning to your base (GemHQ). Navigate efficiently using minimal fuel. Your choices affect your mission score!

How to Play

  • Click each planet once to visit it and plan your route.
  • Plan a full loop — start and end at the same planet.
  • Lines will connect your path. Fuel bar shows how much energy you're burning.
  • After finishing, you'll get a full mission report!
